  • Portrait: photorealistic, elegant headshot, softbox lighting, clean studio background, sharp focus, 4K, professional quality

Tips

Negative Prompts

Use to remove noise: "no watermark, no text, no extra limbs, no distortions, no artifacts".

Sizes and Layouts (Photos)

Specify aspect ratio: 1:1 (square), 3:2 (classic), 4:5 (Instagram), 16:9 (wide), 9:16 (stories).

Lighting

Golden hour, softbox, rim lighting, backlighting, dramatic shadows, volumetric light.

Composition

Rule of thirds, centered composition, leading lines, negative space, symmetry.

Glossary

Term Description
DOF (Depth of Field)Controls how much of the background is blurred versus in focus.
BokehAesthetic quality of the out-of-focus blur (lights and highlights).
Rim LightingEdge light outlining the subject, creating a bright contour.
Volumetric LightVisible light rays through particles, adding atmospheric depth.
Color GradingAdjusting colors and contrasts to create a specific mood/aesthetic.
GimbalStabilizing support for smooth camera movements.
ParallaxDepth effect when layers move at different speeds.
Aspect RatioThe proportion between width and height (e.g., 16:9, 4:5, 1:1, 9:16).
HDRHigh Dynamic Range, expands detail in bright and dark areas.
LUTLook-Up Table, a color grading preset for videos.

Professional Tips for Effective Prompts

Enhance your visual prompts with these professional tips:

Lighting Techniques

Use specific lighting terms like 'golden hour', 'soft box lighting', 'rim lighting', or 'dramatic shadows' for better results.

Camera Angles & Composition

Specify angles like 'wide shot', 'close-up', 'low angle', or composition rules like 'rule of thirds', 'centered composition'.

Style & Quality Descriptors

Include quality terms like 'high detail', 'photorealistic', '4K', 'award-winning', or style terms like 'digital art', 'oil painting'.

Subject Description

Be specific about subject details including pose, expression, clothing, and physical characteristics for accurate representation.

Glossary of Keywords for Expressions and Gestures

Keyword Description
Surprised Character shows a sudden, unexpected reaction with wide eyes and raised eyebrows.
Comforting Character offers support or reassurance through gentle gestures and a calm demeanor.
Excited Character displays enthusiasm and energy with animated movements and a joyful expression.
Thoughtful Character appears to be in deep contemplation, often with a furrowed brow and a focused gaze.
Disappointed Character shows sadness or dissatisfaction with slumped posture and a downcast expression.

Negative Prompts and Examples

Negative prompts help refine your visuals by specifying elements to avoid. Here are some examples:

Image Quality Issues

Exclude: 'blurry', 'low resolution', 'pixelated', 'poor quality', 'distorted', 'artifacts' to ensure crisp results.

Unwanted Elements

Avoid unwanted features by specifying 'no watermarks', 'no text', 'no logos', 'no extra limbs', 'no duplicates'.

Style Exclusions

Control style by excluding 'cartoon style', 'anime style', 'black and white' when you want photorealistic results.
